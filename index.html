GOFAMINT HYMNAL

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2025

Thank you for using GOFAMINT Hymnal, developed by Victor Ajibade. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

GOFAMINT Hymnal is designed to work completely offline and does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or contact details
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behaviour tracking
  • Any financial or payment information

2. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The app saves certain preferences only on your device using local storage. This data never leaves your device and includes:

  • Your selected theme (dark, light, or system)
  • Your chosen font and text size preferences
  • Hymns you have marked as favourites
  • Recently viewed hymns

You can clear this data at any time from within the app via Settings → Clear Recent Hymns.

3. Internet Access

GOFAMINT Hymnal does not require an internet connection to function. All 442 hymns are bundled directly within the app. No data is sent to any server when you use the app.

4. Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate any third-party advertising networks, analytics services, or social media SDKs. No third party has access to any data through this app.

5. Children's Privacy

GOFAMINT Hymnal is suitable for users of all ages, including children. Because we do not collect any personal information, there are no special risks to children using this app.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy in the future, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
